Tonight We Ride Changing the World, One Person At a Time
Watch Our First Video

At Tonight We Ride, our mission is to serve individuals and families in need through faith, compassion, and human connection.

We believe every person is created with dignity and purpose, and that through kindness, service, and community outreach, we can bring hope, healing, and practical support to those facing hardship.

Through faith-based programs and hands-on service, we seek to meet physical, emotional, and spiritual needs — changing the world, one person at a time.

What We Do

Tonight We Ride runs direct, relationship-first outreach in Dallas through three core programs: homeless outreach, veteran support, and emergency relief response. We deliver practical aid quickly, then stay present long enough to build trust and connect people to stable next steps.

The Heart of Tonight We Ride

Tonight We Ride exists to restore dignity, hope, and human connection to people often overlooked—beginning in Dallas.

This is achieved not just through charity, but through relationship, consistency, and presence.

Annual Toy Drive

For 26 years, our Christmas Toy Drive has brought Dallas together to share holiday cheer and support local families. What began as a simple idea has grown into a beloved tradition-powered by community, live music, and the spirit of giving. And we're proud to keep it going year after year.

Crisis Relief & Benefit Concert Series

When crisis strikes — whether it’s a storm, fire, or local tragedy — our community shows up. The Crisis Benefit Concert Series brings people together through live music and generosity to support families and neighbors when they need it most.
