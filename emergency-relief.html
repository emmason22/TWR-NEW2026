Emergency Relief

Submit an Emergency Request

When a crisis hits, waiting weeks for help can mean everything falls apart. Guided by faith and compassion, this fund exists to respond quickly—meeting urgent needs with dignity, grace, and human connection.

Use this form to request assistance or to support the fund and help serve others in their moment of need.

Requests are reviewed with confidentiality and care.

Emergency Response Model

Our team triages requests by urgency, safety risk, and stability impact, then routes support through direct aid and trusted partner referrals.

What to Include

Share timing, current safety concerns, and any immediate barriers so we can respond quickly and appropriately.
